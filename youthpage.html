Youth Unemployment Crisis

Young people face unprecedented challenges entering the job market, with barriers including lack of experience, economic instability, and skills mismatches.



Global Impact: Over 73 million young people are unemployed worldwide, with rates 3x higher than adult unemployment.



Key Challenges: Limited work experience, economic downturns, automation replacing entry-level jobs, inadequate career guidance, and regional economic disparities.

Child Abuse & Neglect

Millions of children and adolescents worldwide experience physical, emotional, or sexual abuse, often by trusted adults, leaving lasting psychological and physical scars.



Shocking Reality: 1 in 4 children experience physical abuse, and 1 in 5 girls and 1 in 13 boys experience sexual abuse.



Forms Include:Physical violence, emotional manipulation, sexual exploitation, neglect, cyberbullying, and exploitation through child labor.

Trauma & PTSD

Young people exposed to violence, natural disasters, war, abuse, or severe accidents often develop trauma responses that affect their development and wellbeing.



Hidden Epidemic:Up to 70% of youth have experienced at least one traumatic event, with many cases going unrecognized.



Common Triggers: Violence, accidents, natural disasters, abuse, witnessing harm to others, sudden loss, and community violence.

Mental Health Crisis

Depression, anxiety, eating disorders, and self-harm rates among youth have reached alarming levels, exacerbated by social media, academic pressure, and global uncertainties.



Critical Numbers: 1 in 5 adolescents experience mental health disorders, with suicide being the 2nd leading cause of death among 15-29 year-olds.



Contributing Factors: Academic pressure, social media comparison, family dysfunction, bullying, identity struggles, and societal expectations.

Bullying & Cyberbullying

Traditional bullying has evolved into 24/7 digital harassment, affecting millions of young people and contributing to anxiety, depression, and academic decline.



Widespread Problem: 1 in 3 students report being bullied at school, and 15% experience cyberbullying online.



Modern Forms: Social exclusion, physical intimidation, verbal harassment, online shaming, doxxing, and targeted harassment campaigns.

Global Youth Displacement

Climate change, conflict, and economic instability force millions of young people to leave their homes, disrupting education and increasing vulnerability.



Staggering Scale: Over 35 million children are displaced globally, many separated from families and lacking access to basic services.



Root Causes: Armed conflict, natural disasters, persecution, climate change, and extreme poverty forcing families to migrate.
